what's decora?

Decora fashion is a playful and colorful style that's characterized by layering bright clothing, vibrant accessories, and an abundance of cute, eye-catching decorations. The style emphasizes self-expression, with wearers often adorning themselves with dozens of hair clips, bracelets, pins, and stickers. Decora outfits commonly feature bold patterns like polka dots and stripes, paired with oversized t-shirts, skirts, or baggy pants in various bright hues. Makeup is often simple but playful, highlighting a youthful appearance that lets the accessories take center stage. Hairstyles are usually dyed in pastel or neon colors and styled to add volume and texture for an exaggerated effect. This whimsical fashion has a DIY spirit, with many enthusiasts customizing their accessories to reflect personal interests. Decora fashion remains an enduring part of Japanese street style, inspiring fans worldwide with its joyfully maximalist aesthetic.

rachel's rating

I love how insanely colorful decora fashion is, and it instantly makes me feel happy and nostalgic. I think it's interesting how creative and versitile this style is in which people can use clothes and accessories from almost anywhere or make their own. While it wouldn't be very practical for me to wear this style everyday (also wearing all those accessories would probably make me overstimulated lol), the maximalist, cluttered look is very visually appealing to me. When I was looking at outfit pictures, I thought it was interesting how some of the clothes and accessories included Western cartoon characters like Spongebob, Sesame Street, and Disney princesses.
